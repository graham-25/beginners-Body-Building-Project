Dum-Bell Exercise Method: Bicep-Curl

  1. Stand up straight with 2 reasonable weighted dum-bells in either hand at your side:
  2. Start by bending your right elbow and lifting the weight towards your right shoulder:
  3. Once the weight is as high as you can get it, hold it there for 1 second:
  4. Slowly release and lower the dumbell back to your side:
  5. Once you have returned to the starting position, now lift the opposite arm in the same manor:
  6. Remember you want to count 2 seconds on the lift, hold for 1 second and release for 3 seconds:
